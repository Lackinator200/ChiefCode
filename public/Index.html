What are ChiefCode?

ChiefCode is a platform that provides a wide range of courses and resources about game development for all game developers around the world. You need the courses,

we got them!

What do we provide?

Wide courses

We provide a wide range of courses for all game developers. You can find courses about game engines, programming languages, and game design.

Learn, try and do

We also provide a option where instead of reading and listening on what it is, and what to do, we do with experiences and quiz to test it!

level progress

We provide a level progress system that allows you to track your progress and see how far you have come. You can also earn badges and rewards for completing courses. (unavailable for now)

Why we exist?

Our journey, as ChiefCode team, is to make progress to all players around a world a goal on making their own game with no struggle

We also provide with professionals, from scripting, to making objects for the game, to publsih them to the world.

We are here to help you, and we are here to make your game a reality, a playable game where you can confess your story characters to the characters you can control!

What do we provide?

scripting

We offer dozens of lessons on coding languages, from C++, C#, Python, Lua, Javascript, and more, along with what their purpose is, and how to make your own.

modelling

We teach everyone on how to design their own 3d models from any website, from Blender, Maya, and much more.

gamewriter

We provide a simple and wide range of game planning, and writting. You can learn on how to create any planning on what your game is/should/will include (basically what kind of updates you want to add).

material maker

We provide a lesson on how you can add materials to your game objects.

Animator

We provide a wide range of animation softwares, from Blender, Maya, and more. You can learn on how to use these softwares, and how to create animation for your cutscenes, movement, and much more.

Marketer

We provide a wide range of marketing tools, including social media, email marketing, and more. You can learn on how to market your game, and how to reach your audience.

Audio maker

We provide a wide range of audio tools, including sound effects, music, and more in multiple softwares like FL studio, Audacity, and more. You can learn on how to create audio for your object, character, or scenes, and how to put it in your game.
